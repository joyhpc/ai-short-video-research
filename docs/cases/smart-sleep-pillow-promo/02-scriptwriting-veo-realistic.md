# 第 2 步：脚本撰写 (Veo 写实版)

## 为什么改为写实方向？
由于过于抽象的隐喻（如琴弦断裂、沙子波动）在纯 AI 视频生成（尤其是 Grok）中容易产生形态崩溃或“不可名状”的扭曲。
针对 **Veo** 的特性：**Veo 极度擅长生成连贯的真人动作、逼真的物理质感、以及细腻的光影变化**。因此，我们完全抛弃抽象概念，直接采用**最直白、最写实的高端生活方式（方向二）**。

---

## Veo 专属分镜头脚本 (6秒/段，无缝衔接)

### Shot 1 (0-6秒)：极度疲惫的真实痛点 (Hook)
*   **【镜头】**：中景，电影感。
*   **【画面 Prompt】**：`Cinematic medium shot. A 35-year-old professional man wearing a slightly loosened dress shirt sits on the edge of a luxurious modern bed at night. He looks completely exhausted, heavily rubbing his stiff neck and closing his eyes in pain. Dim, cool-toned ambient bedroom lighting. Highly realistic, 4k resolution, shot on 35mm lens.`
*   **【旁白】**：卸下一天的硬壳，脖颈依然紧绷？

### Shot 2 (6-12秒)：接触与沉降 (Build)
*   **【镜头】**：特写，慢动作。
*   **【画面 Prompt】**：`Cinematic close-up. The exhausted man slowly lays his head back, sinking deeply into a sleek, premium dark grey smart pillow. As the memory foam gently yields to his weight, the room's lighting subtly and naturally shifts from cold to a warm, comforting amber glow. Realistic fabric texture, shallow depth of field, 4k.`
*   **【旁白】**：当你躺下，一切开始瓦解。

### Shot 3 (12-18秒)：写实的物理舒缓 (Body 1)
*   **【镜头】**：面部微距特写。
*   **【画面 Prompt】**：`Macro close-up shot of the man's face resting comfortably on the dark grey pillow. We can visually see his facial muscles and jawline completely relaxing as he lets out a deep sigh of relief. A very subtle, realistic massage-like vibration is visible on the edge of the pillow fabric. Warm cinematic lighting, highly detailed skin texture, incredibly peaceful.`
*   **【旁白】**：纯净五十赫兹体感振波，揉碎肌肉深处的疲惫。

### Shot 4 (18-24秒)：深睡沉浸 (Body 2)
*   **【镜头】**：全景，静谧推镜头。
*   **【画面 Prompt】**：`Wide cinematic shot of the man sleeping profoundly on the smart pillow in the luxurious, minimalist bedroom. He is completely motionless and at peace. Soft, warm golden hour morning light gently filters through the sheer curtains, illuminating the room. Serene, high-end hotel aesthetic, photorealistic, slow camera push-in.`
*   **【旁白】**：这不是睡眠，这是一次头等舱级的感官 SPA。

### Shot 5 (24-30秒)：高级产品定格 (CTA)
*   **【镜头】**：产品特写，平滑移动。
*   **【画面 Prompt】**：`Elegant commercial product shot. The minimalist dark grey smart pillow sits perfectly on pristine white luxury bedsheets. A soft, warm cinematic light illuminates its ergonomic curves. Slow, smooth camera dolly-out. Empty space on the left side of the frame for text. Luxury tech aesthetic, hyper-realistic.`
*   **【旁白】**：全新智能减压枕。你的私人沉浸舱。
